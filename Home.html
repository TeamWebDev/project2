Salary: Full-time $106,925 + Salary Sacrifice
Agency: Disability Advocacy Service Inc., Alice Springs

Job Summary:

We are searching for a dynamic and experienced leader to join a growing disability advocacy service in the role of Chief Executive Officer (CEO). The CEO will work closely with the Board of Directors, serving as managing director of corporate operations and as the main link between the organisation, government, and non-government stakeholders. The ideal candidate for this position must have a strong commitment to social justice and human rights for people with disability. The candidate must also have previous corporate management experience and a proven track record for effective team management and results-driven leadership of a not-for-profit organisation.
